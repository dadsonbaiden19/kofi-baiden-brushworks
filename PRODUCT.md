# Kofi Baiden Brushworks

## Register

brand

## Purpose

A premium contemporary artist portfolio for Kofi Baiden Brushworks. The site should feel like an online exhibition catalogue: calm, editorial, museum-like, and easy to navigate on mobile.

## Audience

- Collectors and curators
- Galleries and exhibition partners
- Press and collaborators
- Commission and acquisition inquiries

## Positioning

Kofi Baiden is a Ghana-based contemporary visual artist working across painting, drawing, and mixed media.

## Tone

- Minimal luxury
- Strong whitespace
- Refined typography
- Quiet confidence
- Understated, not ecommerce-like

## Must Include

- Authenticity: "All artworks come with an authenticity certificate."
- Worldwide shipping: "Worldwide shipping available. Reach out for a custom shipping quote to your region."

## Avoid

- Tech-startup aesthetics
- Flashy animations
- Aggressive gradients or loud color
- Photography language or marketplace styling
